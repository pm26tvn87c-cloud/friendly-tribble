RECOVERY WATCH
Cyber Intervention Dashboard for Addiction Recovery
Vision
Recovery Watch is a real-time digital intervention platform that monitors public online spaces where individuals are struggling with substance use and enables trained teams to reach them with compassionate support, resources, and wellness checks — before it’s too late.
The Problem
People in active addiction often post publicly on forums, Reddit, TikTok, Instagram, Discord, and other platforms — glorifying use, sharing highs, or crying out for help. Traditional intervention methods miss these critical moments.
Our Solution
A centralized dashboard that allows small teams to:

Monitor high-risk posts in real time
Score risk levels using drug slang triggers
Reach individuals across multiple platforms (Zoom, Telegram, Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, Discord, etc.)
Log cases, coordinate team responses, and track outcomes
Fund interventions through a dedicated NFT collection

Key Features (Demo Available)

Live Alerts Feed — Real-time detection with risk scoring (Critical / High / Medium)
Outreach Center — One-click contact via Zoom, Telegram, Instagram, Snapchat, Messenger, etc.
Case Management — Detailed logging and team collaboration
NFT Collection — “Latest NFT Tracker” with recovery-themed art (100% proceeds fund interventions)
Geographic Hotspots — Visual map of high-risk areas
Team Dashboard — Overview, statistics, and quick actions
